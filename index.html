About Us

More Than Just
A Haircut

The Zone Barbershop is Fort McMurray's go-to spot for fresh cuts, clean fades, and good vibes. Located in the heart of Timberlea, we've built a reputation for delivering top-tier grooming in an atmosphere that feels like home.

Sports on the TVs, a game of Foosball while you wait, and a crew that treats every client like family. Whether you're rolling in after a long shift or getting lined up for a night out, we've got you covered.
